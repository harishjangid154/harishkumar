HAIT(Help Ambulances In Traffic)

A major problem is faced by indian ambulances is that they struck in traffic in emergency.

So here we comeup with a solution for this problem that is HAIT. HAIT is a device that allow ambulance pass through heavy traffic in case of emergency. Only thing the driver have to do is that he/she have to trun on HAIT in case of emergency. HAIT find safest and fastest route for the ambulance to the nearest hospital and if there is traffic in the way it sends a signal to every traffic signal which are in route so that traffic police clear the traffic or the way for ambulance.
